The Timeline of RL Ireland

Origins

RLI was created in somewhat controversial circumstances, of course, we couldn’t have it any other way could we? This is the story as far as we know. Two lads from Donegal called Joseph and Darcy (you may have heard of them) were in 3rd year of secondary school and didn’t know many people around them who played Rocket League. So Darcy has the idea to create Rocket League Ireland, a place where all Irish Rocket League players can come together and play together, a place where they could find teammates and hold tournaments, he told Joseph about this…… and then Joseph got home first and made the Facebook page. And that is how RLI began.
